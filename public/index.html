How to Connect

1
Open this page in two separate browser tabs or devices
2
In the first tab, click "Create Connection Offer" and copy the generated text from "Local Device"
3
In the second tab, paste the copied text into the "Remote Device" area
4
Click "Establish Connection" in the second tab. This will generate new text in its "Local Device" area. Copy this new text.
5
Paste this new text back into the first tab's "Remote Device" area and click "Establish Connection"
6
Once "Connected to peer" appears on both tabs, you can securely transfer files!
